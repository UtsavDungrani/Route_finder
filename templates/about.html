About EcoRoute Finder

Making sustainable travel choices easier and more accessible for everyone.

Our Mission

We believe that every journey can be an opportunity to reduce our environmental impact. Our platform helps you discover the most eco-friendly travel routes while providing transparent information about carbon emissions.

How It Works

Route Analysis

We analyze multiple transportation options between your start and end points using advanced routing algorithms.

Emission Calculation

We calculate carbon emissions for each route using scientifically validated emission factors.

Comparison & Ranking

Routes are ranked by environmental impact, helping you choose the most sustainable option.

Visual Results

Get detailed maps, directions, and environmental impact information for your chosen route.

Emission Factors

Our emission calculations are based on scientific research and industry standards. We consider factors such as:

  • Vehicle type and efficiency
  • Fuel type and consumption
  • Distance and route conditions
  • Passenger occupancy for transit
